Allied Body & Frame has been owned and operated by Peter Brisbo since 1982. For almost 30 years, the same dedicated core team has been providing the highest quality results to our customers. We take the utmost pride in our work and go above and beyond to ensure customer satisfaction.

Insurance

Getting in an accident is stressful. We understand this, and want to make the repair process as smooth and painless as possible for our valued customers. With almost 30 years of experience in the industry, we know a thing or two about dealing with insurance negotiations. We can assist you with insurance company communications and protocol, as needed, to save you the headache.

AAA-insured? Allied Body & Frame is a provider in AAA’s Direct Repair Network and has earned its designation as a AAA Premier Facility. We utilize digital imagery and electronically communicate directly with AAA, which helps expedite the claims process.

Not to worry if you have another insurance carrier or chose not to utilize your insurance company. Rest assured that you’ll receive the same care and assistance that Allied Body & Frame is known for!

Credentials & Awards

Allied Body & Frame is proud to be the recipients of the following awards and recognitions.

I-CAR Gold Class Professionals

Allied Body & Frame is dedicated to keeping our technicians up-to-date on the most current vehicle technology and repair procedures. We are a I-CAR Gold Class Professional Business, one of the highest recognitions available to businesses in the collision repair industry. Businesses who earn this designation not only achieve a high level of training for each employee, but also maintain annual training to provide their customers with efficient, safe, and high-quality services.

AAA Premier Facility

Allied Body & Frame is proud to be a AAA Premier Facility in the Direct Repair Network. The Premier Facility designation is awarded to shops that consistently go above and beyond when it comes to repairs and customer service. Direct Repair Network shops are evaluated on the quality of their repairs, how fast they finish a repair and their overall customer service. No more than the top 15 percent of shops in the Direct Repair Network achieve the designation of Premier Facility.

2011 Premier Achiever Award Winner

Now in its 10th year, the annual Premier Achiever Awards honor auto body shops that consistently achieve high customer satisfaction scores as measured by AutocheX. The class of 2011 Premier Achievers includes top-performing collision repair facilities of all sizes, from small, family-run businesses to large consolidators. In an industry of nearly 50,000 shops in North America, Allied Body and Frame is one of only 400 Premier Achiever Award winners in 2011. We are the only auto body shop in Berkeley, CA to receive this prestigious award.
