About Allied Body & Frame

Peter Brisbo became the owner of Allied Body & Frame in 1982, after working as manager for the business. The shop has thrived over the past 25 years, with the same core team providing the highest quality results to customers. Subscribers to checkbook.org may view our rating of being one of the top auto body shops in the Bay Area.

Personnel

Along with the multiple years of management experience, all of the Allied Body & Frame technicians are kept current with training from I/CAR Collision Repair Training and ASE (National Institute of Automotive Service Excellence). The technicians also undergo training to ensure proper use of on-site equipment and products.

Staying Current

On January 2009, a California mandate will take effect replacing solvent-borne auto body repair paints with water-borne paints. Allied Body & Frame takes pride in being the first shop in Berkeley to comply with this mandate.

Allied has completely integrated the PPG Envirobase paint system into its operations. PPG Envirobase was chosen by Allied because of its historic industry reputation of excellent cutting-edge research, offering unparalleled color match with lower VOC emissions.

Insurance

With 25 years of experience in the industry, Allied Body & Frame has expertise in dealing with insurance negotiations. Allied assists with communication and protocol with insurance companies, as needed.

AAA-insured? Allied Body & Frame is a provider in the AAA Direct Repair Network and is a AAA Direct Repair Facility. We utilize digital imagery and electronically communicate directly with AAA, thereby expediting claims. A car rental car facility is conveniently located within walking distance of our facility, and pick-up and return service is available.

Those choosing not to utilize their insurance company can rest assured that Allied offers the same care and assistance to ensure customer satisfaction.
