Since 1974, Allied Body & Frame has been committed to providing quality auto body services to the Bay Area community. Allied Body & Frame prides itself on its trusted reputation and dedication to top-quality work.

Hassle–free experience.

Allied Body & Frame repairs a majority of Asian and European cars, along with a good percentage of domestics. We are conveniently located in the ever-growing West Berkeley business district, surrounded by 4th Street Shopping, Westbrae and REI Shopping Center.

We stand by all of our work and we are committed to providing customers with a hassle—free experience.
